About me

Hi I'm Shabiib,

Founder and Specialist Maths Tutor and Mentor at MathTutorInsights.

For over 13 years I have helped students aged 11 to 19 build confidence, improve grades, and feel like maths finally clicks. Families call me part tutor and part coach because I teach both the methods and the mindset.

Having helped hundreds of students move up 3 to 5 grades in GCSE,IGCSE and A-level. It includes student passing after a resit or moved up sets. Teachers and headteachers in the education field have chosen me to support their own child outside the classroom.

I began tutoring at 16 at the start of my A levels. My path wasn’t perfect. I failed my first year at university, rebuilt, and graduated with a 2:1 in maths with a year in industry. I also played professional basketball as a teenager and later stepped away from the professional game to focus fully on my maths degree. That journey shaped the calm; step-by-step way I teach and strengthened the discipline and coaching style I bring to every lesson.

Tutoring has always been my first choice, not a step after classroom teaching. Consistent student progress and the trust of parents and teachers has kept me committed to delivering impact.

I stay active with weekly training, which keeps my energy high in lesson. I’ve also travelled across Europe, the Middle East, Asia and Africa. This has helped connect more with students and parents.

Who is this programme for?

Our math programmes are carefully designed for students struggling with the 5 common problems below. If any of these sound familiar, you're in the right place!

Gaps in Subject Knowledge

Gaps are feedback to a student that their maths is not connecting. We connect new knowledge to old, like a puzzle, making concepts unforgettable.

Lack of Focus & Interest

This occurs from low connection and trust from student with a tutor. We develop their focus and interset for parents to see real changes in their childs studying and hear what they learnt each lesson.

Falling Behind in Class

Whether it's fast-paced topics or time away from school, we help students catch up and move ahead, building confidence every step of the way.

Busy Schedules

Busy with sports or hobbies? As a former athlete, students get first hand experience the best learning practise to excel in maths whilst doing what they love.

Weak Exam Instincts

Students may have the knowledge but do not know where to start on an exam question or determine the final steps in the working out to reach the answers. We train them.

Our 4 Programme Pillars for Student Success

Pillar 1

Problem Awareness

Your child is stuck and not sure why. Our short skills check finds root causes for missing methods, confusing exam words and low confidence. Clear targets provided to begin the journey of progress

Pillar 2

Academic & Human Connection

We reconnect the dots so maths feels smaller and clearer. Your child opens up, shares worries, and feels heard. They no longer feel alone or lost seeing how topics link

Pillar 3

Topic Acceleration

From topic practice to full exam papers. Variation meets accuracy and speed, so your child can answer any question style within every topic with time to check workings.

Pillar 4

Grade Retention
